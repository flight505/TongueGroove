Tongue & Groove

Creates parametric tongue-and-groove joints between two bodies along a sketch centreline path. Designed for 3D printing with configurable fit clearances.

Quick Start

  1. Draw a centreline sketch on the tongue body face
  2. Click Solid → Modify → Tongue & Groove
  3. Select the centreline, tongue body, and groove body
  4. Adjust dimensions and clearances
  5. Click OK

Fit Clearance

  • Side — groove wider than tongue per side (0.1–0.2mm for FDM)
  • Bottom — groove deeper than tongue
  • End — tongue shorter than groove per end

End Behaviour

  • Inset (default) — end clearance applied, optional pullback distance
  • Flush — no clearance, joint goes to path endpoint (use at body edges)

Tips

  • Draw the centreline on the tongue body, not the groove body
  • Click one curve to auto-select the full connected path
  • Use Inset with 0mm for paths ending inside the body
  • Use Flush only where the path exits through a body edge
